The MethodFinder is also in the parts bin. Open the parts bin from the world menu, search for "MethodFinder" and pull it out.
How to open it?
You can open an MethodFinder by marking text or clicking in the world and pressing CMD+F
The Method Finder does a fulltext search of methods in classes, objects and traits in the running system. It then can browse these messages and open and Source Code Browser or Object Editor for further work.
